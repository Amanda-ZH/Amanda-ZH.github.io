My name is Zhang Ruoye , a 15-year-old girl . I am lively and outgoing .

I am now a student in Jingling High School, where my curiosity for learning new things is satisfied.I am a very happy girl . I always talk with my parents about the books which I recently read . And we will discuss our opinions , sometimes we found the thoughts different ,and I will tell them why I think so .My parents always says that I am a teenager with independent thinking and have ability to judge whether something is right or not . My parents also think I am a person who love the life very much , because I can always notice the small love around me and my neighbors said that I am also a person who is loving.As I always feed the stray cat and willing to help disabled person .I have a lot of friends because I am a gregarious person . My friends always find me great-hearted that I can always face difficulties by myself and deal it bravely . In their opinion ,I am full of confidence . I like lecture , host ,and like to show my ability to everyone . I am fond of writing. I think it is a creative form to keep memory alive. That’s also my way of recording life. I am interested in novels , and because I am good at writing .I always write some short stories when I am free. I’d love to write some stories with traditional Chinese culture .I sometimes give them to my friends to read ,and they always find my novels not only interesting but also connotative. In the future ,I hope my people can read my article.
